Shrimp Steak Recipe

Thought our dry steak recipe was great? Now try it with ocean's olives we know as Shrimp!

Ingredients:

  • 8oz Steak of Desired Cut
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 4oz of Shrimp

Steps:

  1. Set heat of frying pan to low and add cooking grease.
  2. Throw a pinch of salt on each side of your steak & Shrimp.
  3. Cook each side of your steak for 15 minutes.
  4. Then throw in your shrimp. Gently flipping shrimp after every minute.
  5. Check shrimp to insure dryness, then serve!
