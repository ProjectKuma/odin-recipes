Dry Steak Recipe

Hate it when your steak is pink, or even worse, red in the middle? Look no further as this steak will be anything but juicy.

Ingredients:

  • 8oz Steak of Desired Cut
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Patience

Steps:

  1. Set heat of frying pan to low and add cooking grease.
  2. Throw a pinch of salt on each side of your steak.
  3. Cook on each side for roughly 20 minutes. Make sure your steak is thouroughly dark.
