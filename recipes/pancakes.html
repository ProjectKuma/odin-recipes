Pancakes Recipe

Bisquicks mix is excellent for making homemade style pancakes you'd find at restaurants's like IHOP.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Bisquick Mix
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 2 Eggs

Steps:

  1. Stir incredients until blended.
  2. Pour slightly less than 1/4 cupfuls onto hot greased gridle.
  3. Cook until edges are dry, Turn; cook until golden.
